The 1990's

The 1990s are a Britpop cover band formed in Hull. Playing all your favourite songs from Oasis, Blur, Shed Seven, Ocean Colour Scene, Cast, Paul Weller, Pulp, The Stone Roses and many more.

The 1990s are suitable for all occasions, especially for those who can't leave the classic sound of the 1990s behind. The band has been through a few member changes over the years but are now playing all over the country, with a vibrant set that will have you dancing and reminiscing the night away.
