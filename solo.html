Andy is a musician from Hull and is currently working on his first studio album. He started playing guitar at the age of 14 at Howden School. He studied Performing Arts at Hull college where he learned to sing (thanks to some patient friends). Andy has played in lots of cover bands over the years (Acoustica, Only Natural, Avenue) and currently plays with The Millionaires and The 1990s.

His musical influences come from The Beatles, Crowded House, Dispatch, Shed Seven, White Lies, The Jam and The Eagles. Andy has always had a real love for harmonies and hopes that his album will capture the sounds of some of his favourite artists.
